# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 17 March 2026**

New Life UK ("the app", "we") is a free, open-source, non-commercial project. This page explains what data the app handles, why, and your rights under UK GDPR.

---

## 1. Who we are

**Data controller:** New Life UK  
**Contact:** hello@newlifeuk.org  
**Source code:** https://github.com/na7hanw/new-life-uk

---

## 2. What data the app stores

The app stores data **only on your device** using the browser's `localStorage`. Nothing is ever sent to our servers. The keys we write are:

| Key | Purpose |
|-----|---------|
| `nluk_lang` | Your language preference |
| `nluk_dark` | Dark / light mode choice |
| `nluk_wtab` | Last work tab viewed |
| `nluk_tx3` | Cached auto-translations (speeds up repeat visits) |
| `nluk_consent` | Your crash-report consent choice (see section 4) |

You can delete all of this data at any time from **Settings → Clear all app data**, or by clearing your browser's site storage.

---

## 3. What we do NOT collect

- No analytics
- No cookies
- No user accounts or profiles
- No IP addresses or location data
- No personal information of any kind

The app is fully static (no server, no database). There is no mechanism for us to receive or store your data.

---

## 4. Optional crash reports (Sentry)

With your explicit permission, the app may send **anonymous** error reports to [Sentry](https://sentry.io) when it crashes. These reports contain only:

- The JavaScript error message and stack trace
- Browser type and version
- App version

They do **not** contain any personal information, session recordings, or user identifiers.

You will be asked for consent before any report is sent. You can change your choice at any time in **Settings → Privacy & Data**. See Sentry's own privacy policy at [sentry.io/privacy/](https://sentry.io/privacy/).

---

## 5. Your UK GDPR rights

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018 you have the right to:

- **Access** — know what data is held about you
- **Erasure** — have your data deleted (use the "Clear all app data" button in Settings)
- **Portability** — receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Object** — object to processing
- **Complain** — lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)

Because the app stores data only on your own device, you can exercise most of these rights yourself instantly. To exercise rights over any crash-report data held by Sentry, email us at hello@newlifeuk.org.

**ICO complaints:** [ico.org.uk/make-a-complaint/](https://ico.org.uk/make-a-complaint/)
